What is OneTwoSport?

OneTwoSport offers a blend of performance and usability that produces high precision, biomechanically-relevant motion capture data via experiences.

Each sports room (i.e. baseball, ice hockey, cricket and others) is prepared as much as possible identical to professional sports with the use of professional equipment. Our main target group is families and children. The main element of the proposal for schools and parents will be the presentation and education (mostly through practice) of professional sports.
