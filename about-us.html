FAQ

  • Do I need to book in advance?

    You don't, but it's definitely recommended - especially on the weekends. You can learn whether there are spots available for our activities on site at our reception desk.

  • I have a reservation - how early should I come?

    It's best to arrive at least 20 minutes prior to your booking in order to start on time. If you come even earlier, you will have more time to order something at one of our restaurant!

  • Is there a private parking?

    If you're planning on coming by car, there are plenty of parking spots nearby.

  • Do I need to know the rules of the activity I booked?

    Yes, you do, but that's not a problem as our trained staff can teach you everything on site! All of our activities take less than 2 minutes to learn.

  • Do you accept large group bookings?

    Of course! We have a space of over 1500m2 with tons of different options. For groups of up to 8 people, we recommend booking our activities. For larger groups, it's best to contact us via contact form or phone in order to determine all the details in advance - for more information, please see the Reservation tab.
